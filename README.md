# 🌱 EcoAir – Real-Time Air Quality & Environmental Impact Tracker

🌍EcoAir is a comprehensive web application designed to empower individuals to monitor air quality, track their environmental impact, and contribute to sustainability efforts. Think of it as a **fitness tracker for the planet** – helping users reduce their carbon footprint while staying informed about local air quality conditions.

Developed as the final project for **Harvard University's CS50x 2025**, this application demonstrates proficiency in full-stack web development, database design, and user experience implementation.

## ✨ Features

### 🌤️ Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring
- Search air quality by city name
- Color-coded AQI indicators (Green/Yellow/Red)
- Historical air quality data visualization
- Health recommendations based on pollution levels

### 📊 Environmental Impact Dashboard
- Track trees planted with CO₂ reduction calculations
- Monitor energy savings and carbon offset
- Log sustainable transportation usage
- Set and achieve environmental goals

### 🎮 Gamified Progress System
- Earn points for eco-friendly actions
- Unlock achievements and badges
- Leaderboard for community engagement
- Visual progress tracking

### 💼 Carbon Offset Marketplace
- Browse verified environmental projects
- Calculate carbon footprint
- Support reforestation, renewable energy, and conservation
- Transparent impact reporting

### 🔋 Renewable Energy Tools
- Solar panel savings calculator
- Green energy provider comparisons
- Energy consumption analytics
- ROI estimation tools

### ♻️ Waste Management Assistant
- Recycling/composting/disposal guides
- Weekly waste tracking
- Local facility locator
- Reduction goal setting

### 🏢 Green Companies Directory
- Search sustainable businesses
- Filter by category and location
- Company sustainability ratings
- User reviews and recommendations
